Services Offered to you through Ledboine


Insurance

We can help you obtain insurance for any life event, such as travel, health, disability and life insurance. There are many options out there to meet different needs and financial situations. Let us find you the perfect insurance for your needs and lifestyle.



Retirement

It's never too early to start planning for your retirement! We can help you open up a TFSA (tax free savings account), an RRSP (registered retirement savings plan), or an RRIF (registered retirement income fund). All these accounts will assist you in saving money for when you decide to retire. Let us help find the account best for you.


Your Child's Education

Saving for your child's education is easily done through use of a TFSA (tax free savings account) or an RESP (registered education savings plan). Whether you are saving for the future of one child or many, we can guide you in the right direction and help you save enough money to benefit you and your child.


Investing

Investing money is a smart way to increase your capital. By talking to one of our financial advisors about making investments based on the amount you want to contribute as well as your preferred level of risk, we will be able to invest your money the way you want to. Just fill out the form on our contact page and Ledboine will respond with a plan to help with your investments.
